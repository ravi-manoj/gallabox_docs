# How does selecting from a contact group work?

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (108).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Click on the “**Select Group**”.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (110).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* In case you do not have any groups/segments, you can create a group/ segment by clicking on **“Create Group”**

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (107).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* On clicking **“Create Group”** you will be redirected to this pop-up

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (88).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Click on the **“Filter”** and configure the segment/ group as per your requirement.
* You can add one or more filters. The contacts which satisfy all the filter conditions will be displayed below
* We display a maximum of 12 contacts on a page. You can move across pages by clicking on the **“Next”** and **“Previous”** buttons.
* Once you are all happy with the segment/ group you have created. You can provide a Name for it here and click on **“Create Group”**

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (56).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Once you have created the needful segments/groups, Select one or more from the list displayed on this page. These would be the list of customers who will receive your broadcast messages.

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/image (123).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Here you can see the details like segment name, Number of filters, Number of contacts, and name of the person who created the segment/ group.
* Once you have selected the segments/ group, click on **“Continue”** to proceed further.
